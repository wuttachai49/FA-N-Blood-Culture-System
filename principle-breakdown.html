FA-N120 and FA-N240 models include automated sample volume detection ensuring adequate blood for optimal culture recovery.

📈 Bacterial Growth Curve Phases

The FA-N Series detects growth starting from LOG PHASE, enabling early results in 1 hour for rapid growers.

LAG PHASE (0-2 hours)

Bacteria adjust to environment. Minimal cell division. Not detected.

LOGARITHMIC (2-6 hours)

Rapid multiplication. CO₂ production. 1-4 hours

STATIONARY (6-7+ hours)

Growth stabilizes. Confirmed positive

DEATH PHASE (7+ hours)

Cell death. Maintained positive
